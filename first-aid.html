Course Purpose

To provide first aid awareness and basic life support training for emergency situations in domestic and professional environments. This course equips learners with essential skills to handle medical emergencies confidently.

Course Content

  • Wounds and bleeding management techniques
  • Burns and fractures treatment procedures
  • Emergency scene management and safety assessment
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) for adults and children
  • Respiratory distress management (Choking, blocked airway)
  • Basic life support techniques and protocols
  • Emergency response procedures and communication
  • First aid kit preparation and maintenance

Course Benefits

  • Internationally recognized certification
  • Practical hands-on training with real scenarios
  • Experienced certified instructors
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Job placement assistance after completion
  • Lifetime skills that can save lives
