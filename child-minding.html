Course Purpose

To provide comprehensive basic child and baby care training, covering developmental stages, safety protocols, and educational activities for professional childcare services.

Course Content

  • Birth to six-month old baby needs and care
  • Seven-month to one year old developmental needs
  • Toddler care and development activities
  • Age-appropriate educational toys and activities
  • Child safety and emergency procedures
  • Nutrition and feeding requirements by age
  • Sleep routines and healthy habits
  • Communication with parents and caregivers

Course Benefits

  • Professional childcare certification
  • Work in daycare centers or private homes
  • Start your own child minding business
  • Essential skills for parents and caregivers
  • High demand for qualified child minders
  • Flexible working hours possible
