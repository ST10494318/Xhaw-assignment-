Course Purpose

To provide essential skills to navigate basic life necessities, including financial management, legal rights, and fundamental literacy for personal and professional development.

Course Content

  • Opening and managing a bank account
  • Basic labour law and workers' rights
  • Basic reading and writing literacy skills
  • Basic numeric literacy and financial management
  • Budgeting and saving strategies
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Time management and organization
  • Problem-solving and decision-making

Course Benefits

  • Improved financial independence and management
  • Better understanding of employment rights
  • Enhanced communication abilities
  • Increased confidence in daily life tasks
  • Foundation for further education and career growth
  • Practical skills applicable to all aspects of life
