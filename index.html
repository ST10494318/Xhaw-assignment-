About Our Mission

Empowering the Nation was established in 2022 with a vision to transform lives through skills development and professional training.

We provide comprehensive training programs designed specifically for domestic workers and gardeners, empowering them with sustainable skills that lead to better employment opportunities and higher income potential. Our initiative, supported by President Bashkin's skills development program, focuses on creating capable professionals who can offer enhanced services to households across Johannesburg.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our training programs.

What are the class schedules? +
We offer flexible scheduling with morning (8:00-12:00), afternoon (13:00-17:00), and weekend classes (9:00-13:00) to accommodate different needs.
Are the courses certified? +
Yes, all our courses provide nationally recognized certificates upon successful completion. Our certifications are valued by employers across South Africa.
What payment options are available? +
We offer full payment, installment plans, and employer sponsorship options. We also provide discounts for multiple course registrations.
Do you provide job placement assistance? +
Yes, we have partnerships with employment agencies and provide job placement support to all our graduates.
