Course Purpose

To provide comprehensive landscaping services for new and established gardens, covering plant selection, garden design, and structural elements.

Course Content

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees identification
  • Fixed structures installation (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in braai)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden ecosystem
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and color combinations
  • Professional garden layout and design principles
  • Soil preparation and plant nutrition
  • Seasonal garden maintenance planning
  • Client consultation and project management

Course Benefits

  • Start your own landscaping business
  • Work with property developers and homeowners
  • Create beautiful outdoor living spaces
  • Learn sustainable gardening practices
  • High demand for skilled landscapers
  • Creative and physically rewarding work
