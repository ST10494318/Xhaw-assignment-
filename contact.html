Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about visiting our centers

Do I need to make an appointment to visit? +
While appointments are recommended to ensure staff availability, walk-ins are welcome during business hours. We recommend calling ahead for campus tours.
Is parking available at all locations? +
Yes, all our campuses offer free secure parking. Sandton and Johannesburg CBD have underground parking, while Soweto has gated outdoor parking.
Can I register for courses in person? +
Absolutely! You can visit any of our locations to register for courses, make payments, and complete your enrollment with assistance from our staff.
Are your facilities wheelchair accessible? +
Yes, all our campuses are fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restroom facilities.
