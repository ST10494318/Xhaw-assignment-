Course Purpose

To provide basic knowledge of watering, pruning, and planting techniques in domestic gardens, focusing on sustainable practices and plant health maintenance.

Course Content

  • Water restrictions and watering requirements of indigenous and exotic plants
  • Professional pruning and propagation techniques
  • Planting techniques for different plant types
  • Soil preparation and fertilization methods
  • Pest and disease identification and management
  • Seasonal garden maintenance schedules
  • Tool maintenance and safety procedures
  • Weed control and lawn care

Course Benefits

  • Professional garden maintenance skills
  • Work as a gardener for multiple households
  • Start your own garden maintenance business
  • Learn sustainable water-wise gardening
  • Increase your value as a domestic worker
  • Create and maintain beautiful outdoor spaces
